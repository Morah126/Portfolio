Snoopy & the Peanuts

Characters

Snoopy


SUPPERTIME

Snoopy never forgets when it’s time to eat and often celebrates the occasion with a happy dance.

WRITING

As the World Famous Author, Snoopy is forever trying to write the great American novel.

MAKE-BELIEVE

Snoopy loves to fantasize about being various characters. Most notably, the World War I Flying Ace.

Charlie Brown


THE LITTLE RED-HAIRED GIRL

Charlie Brown has a crush on the Little Red-Haired girl. She is never seen in the strip.

BASEBALL

Charlie Brown always looks forward to baseball season and is his team’s manager and pitcher.

KITES

Charlie Brown always tries, but rarely succeeds. He has several run-ins with the “kite eating tree.”

Pig Pen


ARCHAEOLOGY

Pigpen has affixed to him the dirt and dust of past civilizations.

MUD PIES

Violet makes mud-pies and Pigpen is her best customer!

POLITICS

Pigpen aspires to be class president one day.

Woodstock


BEAGLE SCOUTS

Woodstock is Snoopy’s second-in-command when leading hikes with the rest of the Beagle Scouts.

HELICOPTERS

Woodstock is Snoopy’s helicopter pilot.

WORMS

Once his favorite food, Woodstock falls in love with a worm while he’s attending worm school.
